== Description ==

Elevate your website with our all-in-one WordPress plugin, offering mobile-responsive BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate) and BMI (Body Mass Index) calculators.
BMR calculates the minimum calories your body needs at rest to support vital functions, offering insights into metabolic health.
BMI provides a weight category based on height and weight, indicating overall health status.
Easily add these insightful health tools to your site using [bmr_shortcode] and [bmi_shortcode], enhancing user engagement by providing valuable health assessments directly on your platform. Ideal for health and wellness sites, this plugin seamlessly integrates essential health metrics for your audience.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Insert the shortcodes `[bmr_shortcode]` and `[bmi_shortcode]` directly into your pages or posts.
